About VinArb

We all know how important it is to record your thoughts when tasting wine.

Here, you will find a minimally intrusive platform to record notes and upload your wine photos: A database in the cloud.

Simply take a picture, rate, comment, and save. Finish enjoying your wine, and when you come back, you'll find your review paired with a wine in the database.


Just tap the Database to add a note!
